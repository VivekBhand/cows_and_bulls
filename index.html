HOW TO PLAY

Alrighty then, partner! Here's a fun and straightforward guide just for you:

1.The game begins with the computer picking a secret four-digit number for you to crack. No repeating digits allowed, alright?
2.Now it's your turn to guess the secret number. Type in a four-digit number using your keyboard.
3.The computer will give you hints based on your guess. If a digit in your guess matches a digit in the secret number and is in the correct position, the computer will say "Bull!"
4.If a digit in your guess matches a digit in the secret number but is in the wrong position, the computer will say "Cow!"
5.Keep guessing and receiving hints from the computer until you finally corral the secret number. Giddy up!
